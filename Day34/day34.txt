2. Explain Each Step

Collect Data: Gather data from different sources.

Clean Data: Remove errors, duplicates, and missing values.

Train Model: Teach the model using training data.

Test Model: Evaluate the model's performance.

Prediction: Use the trained model to predict outcomes for new data.
