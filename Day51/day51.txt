Task 4 - How ChatGPT Uses NLP

ChatGPT uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to understand and generate human language. It analyzes the user's input, identifies the context and meaning, and produces relevant, accurate, and natural responses. NLP enables ChatGPT to answer questions, translate languages, summarize text, generate content, and hold conversations similar to human communication.
